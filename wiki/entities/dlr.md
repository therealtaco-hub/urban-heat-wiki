# DLR — German Aerospace Center

Germany's national aeronautics and space research centre. Its German Remote Sensing Data Center (*Deutsches Fernerkundungsdatenzentrum*, DFD) in Weßling (near Munich) specialises in satellite data processing including urban LST and land cover products.

## Role in Urban Heat Research

- Partner on the García de León et al. Munich LST+trees study: Tobias Leichtle and Hannes Taubenböck are DLR researchers. *(Source: [[wiki/sources/garcia-de-leon-lst-trees-munich]])*
- DLR produces high-quality downscaled LST products and urban land cover datasets that underpin city-level analysis.
- Hannes Taubenböck leads urban remote sensing work bridging DLR and the University of Würzburg.
