# ETH Zürich — Institute for Atmospheric and Climate Science

Swiss federal technical university. The Institute for Atmospheric and Climate Science produced the Schwaab et al. (2021) Nature Communications study on urban tree cooling across 293 European cities — the largest European-scale evidence base for the 8–12 K tree cooling effect in Central Europe.

## Key Researchers

- **Jonas Schwaab** — lead author
- **Sonia Seneviratne** — climate extremes expertise
- **Edouard L. Davin** — now at University of Bern / Wyss Academy

## Role in Urban Heat Research

Produced the European continental-scale evidence that trees in Central European cities cool LST by 8–12 K relative to urban fabric in summer — the key benchmark for setting cooling expectations in Germany (including Würzburg).
