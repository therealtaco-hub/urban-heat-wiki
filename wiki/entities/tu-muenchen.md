# TU München (Technical University of Munich)

German research university in Munich, home to urban heat and forestry researchers relevant to this wiki.

## Overview

TU München's forestry science department (Forstwissenschaft) is the institutional home of [[wiki/entities/thomas-roetzer]], who developed tree-level cooling quantification methods.

## Key Data / Stats

- Location: Munich, Bavaria, Germany
- Relevant department: Forstwissenschaft (Forest Science)

## Role in Urban Heat Research

- Produced the tree cooling quantification methodology (Rötzer et al.).
- Located within [[wiki/entities/muenchen]] — directly relevant to the city it studies.
